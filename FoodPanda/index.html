How to order on Foodpanda App

To follow this step and get the best offer for you

Step-01

Select "Food Delivery" from foodpanda app home screen.

Step-02

Choose your favourite categories.

Step-03

Choose your favourite restaurant.

Step-04

Choose what to order and select your favorite item.

Step-05

Click "add to cart".

Step-06

Go to the "Cart"

Step-07

"Use voucher" if there is any and click "Review payment and address".

Step-08

After checking delivery address and filling payment details, click "Place Order".

Step-09

When the time is up, receive your order from the delivery man by showing your order number from the app. Enjoy your meal!

Foodpanda is an online food delivery and food ordering platform that allows you to order food from local restaurants and have it delivered to your doorstep.

You can download the Foodpanda app from your device's app store, such as the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

Foodpanda's availability depends on your location. You can check if the service is available in your area by entering your address in the app or on their website.

To place an order, open the app, enter your location, browse restaurants, select items from their menus, and add them to your cart. Once you've made your selections, proceed to checkout, choose your payment method, and confirm your order.

Many food delivery apps, including Foodpanda, offer real-time order tracking. You can see the status of your order and the estimated delivery time.

Delivery fees vary depending on the restaurant and your location. Some restaurants may offer free delivery for certain order amounts.

Foodpanda usually has customer support available through the app or website. You can contact them to resolve any issues with your order or delivery.
